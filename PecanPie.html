Pecan Pie

This pecan pie is just perfect for people who prefer desserts that are not too sweet. This is a family recipe which has been passed on by generations. I have made some alterations to this recipe so that it's not as time consuming and ready to be gobbled up in no time!

Prep Time Cook Time Ready In Serves
10m 1hr 1hr 10m 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked store bought pie shell
  • 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon cold water
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups chopped pecans

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, corn syrup, butter, water, and cornstarch. Bring to a full boil, and remove from heat.
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans. Pour into pie shell.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until filling is set.
