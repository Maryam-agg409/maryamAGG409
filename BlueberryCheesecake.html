Blueberry Cheesecake

Blueberry Cheesecake is one of my and my family's favourite desserts. Below is the recipe for the deliciously rich blueberry cheesecake.

Prep Time Cook Time Ready In Serves
20m 1hr 30m 1hr 50m 10

Ingredients:

  • 100g or 1 stick of melted butter
  • 200g of Digestive biscuits (You can use any other biscuits of your choice)
  • A pinch if salt
  • 200g cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50ml water
  • 2 teaspoon gelatin
  • 2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tin of blueberry pie filling

Directions:

    For the Biscuit Layer

  1. Finely process the biscuits in a grinder.
  2. Place the biscuits in the tray you will be making your cheesecake in and add salt to your liking.
  3. Add in the butter and mix well. Gently press the biscuit layer to even. And refrigerate for 20-30 minutes.

    4. For the Cheese Mixture and Topping

  4. Add the cream cheese to the heavy cream and mix well.
  5. Add the Icing sugar to the cream cheese and heavy cream mixture and beat together with a hand mixer until fully combined.
  6. Add the lemon juice in and mix thoroughly.
  7. Add the gelatin in a cup of water amd microwave for around 40 seconds. Mix the gelatin well and pour it into the mixture.
  8. Pour the mixture over the biscuits base and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  9. Pour the blueberry filling from the tin over the settled cheese cake and refrigerate it again for 1 hour.
  10. Try to leave it in the fridge overnight for best results and enhanced flavour. But if you can't, BON APPETIT!
